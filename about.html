Our Story

Forge began with a vision to create a more integrated set of digital tools. What started as a collection of disparate tools and platforms evolved into Forge, conceived and developed by Nicholas Xander Davis. Frustrated by juggling multiple websites and interfaces, Nicholas launched Forge with Nova — PNG Converter on November 10, 2023, marking the beginning of our journey. Since then, Forge has grown exponentially, expanding to include a wide array of tools designed to meet various digital needs, from file conversions to task management. Our development is characterized by a commitment to continuous improvement, with regular updates and new features enhancing functionality and user experience.
